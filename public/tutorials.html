You are one step closer to clean, radiant skin

Follow the key, easy steps to get going

    There are important things you must do so that the tool can work at a maximum perfomance you want.

    We recommend that you take some time to go through
    each of the following steps for best results:

  1. Use the system right after washing your face with a Sodium Cocoate and
    EDTA soap.
  2. Make sure you are using a camera with at least 12Mp, f/2.0, 27mm wide.

radiant skin

    For first users, steps on how to use this tool are shown below:

  1. Snap a picture of your skin sample with a resolution of at least 5x zoomed in Or upload a picture and click scan
    button.
  2. Then the smart tool will show your skin type and also recommends skin care products that will
    be suitable for your skin.
  3. If your skin type is not detected it should say “Image Error” and you should retake or re-upload
    your skin sample.
